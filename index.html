RENAISSANCE LAW CHAMBERS

Renaissance Law Chambers (RLC) is a full service law firm with a diverse practice. Our team of lawyers have over the years gained extensive experience in advising and resolving disputes across various industries including banking and finance, insurance, commerce, energy, information & communications technology, telecommunications, agriculture, real estate & construction and trusts. The diversity in our repertoire reflects the collective expertise and interests of our legal practitioners, and places us in a distinctive position to synergize our individual strengths in pursuit of the optimum solution for our clients. As our name suggests, we aim at approaching the law in a distinctly revitalised manner. Steered by a youthful, enthusiastic and dynamic legal team, as well as a very determined support staff, we wholeheartedly provide clients with the well-researched, relevant, and pragmatic legal solutions they deserve. It is our aim to be a leading law firm in Ghana.
